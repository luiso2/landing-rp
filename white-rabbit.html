The brief was the calendar.

White Rabbit came to us with the brand fully designed, a confirmed launch weekend, and one constraint: every product had to be in fans' hands within 11 weeks. There was no version of the timeline that allowed for a missed date.

We pulled the discovery, prototyping and tooling phases into the first three weeks. By week four we were running production samples. By week nine the first containers were on the road.

Five SKUs, one system.

The series spans crystal-clear 24oz PET tumblers, a 32oz commemorative cup, two character sippers and a beverage carrier. Each SKU shares the same core toolkit so quality and color stay consistent across the line.

The print program ran live during the launch weekend — limited variants released on a 3-day cadence, manufactured ahead and held in our warehouse for cross-dock.
