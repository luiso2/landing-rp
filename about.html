Built around the calendar.

RP and Associates was founded in 1987 by a small team of designers and manufacturers who believed promotional drinkware deserved better. Better materials. Better print. Better delivery dates.

Three decades later, we still operate on that same principle: every product we make has to be on a shelf — or in a fan's hand — by a fixed date. There are no soft deadlines in our world.

From our Hermosa Beach studio, we work with global brands, regional teams, and one-off events that need a partner who treats their launch like our own.

What we believe.

Three principles that have stayed unchanged since 1987.

Make it personal.

Every project gets a single point of contact. You won't be passed between departments — you'll have one person who knows your brand and your timeline.

Make it well.

We choose materials that last in the field, hold ink properly, and recycle when their job is done. Cheap drinkware is the most expensive drinkware.

Make it on time.

The most beautiful product in the world is worthless if it lands the day after the event. Our promise is the date — everything else is a tool to keep it.
